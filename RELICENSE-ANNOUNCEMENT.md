## XboxPrefill is licensed under the GNU AGPL v3

Hey everyone,

XboxPrefill is released under the **GNU Affero General Public License v3.0
(AGPL-3.0)**.

To be clear up front: it stays **fully open source**. This is a stronger
open-source license, not a move away from open source.

**Nothing changes for the people who actually use this project.** You can still:

- Self-host it, for free, for yourself, your LAN parties, your business, or your
  gaming cafe.
- Read the full source, fork it, and modify it.
- Get every future feature and fix.

**What's new:** if you run a *modified* version for other people (including as a
hosted service), you have to make your modified source available to them under
the same license, with the original copyright intact. In other words,
improvements get shared back, and the project can't be quietly turned into a
closed, proprietary product.

### Details

- XboxPrefill builds on the MIT-licensed lancache-prefill tools by Tim Pilius
  ([@tpill90](https://github.com/tpill90)) and the shared `LancachePrefill.Common`
  library. Those upstream portions remain under the MIT License with their
  original copyright notice; XboxPrefill as a whole is distributed under AGPL-3.0,
  as the MIT License permits.
- The change applies to **all XboxPrefill releases**.
- Full text: [LICENSE](LICENSE).

Why the change? To keep XboxPrefill healthy and open for the long run, and to make
sure improvements flow back to the community instead of disappearing into closed
forks. Thanks for being here, and happy to answer any questions in the comments.

— Regix
